About us

Art Market Serbia is an online gallery, made with the purpose to help Serbian artists to become internationally visible, to sell their artworks in-country and across the border, and to share their professional observations with colleagues and audience. The audience participates through comments, so they indirectly influence the artistic creative process. The connection between artists and their online international spectators is the core of our idea.

Besides those international connections, our focus is on selling art. It is very important for us to make an economical cycle in which artists could be financially stable. That is why along with the online promotion of their work, we organize fundraising once per month, which makes us more visible and creates a base for firm connections with our business partners.

Considering the use of our website, artists create their profiles and arrange their artworks with all information. Also, artists could use our "Artists talk" section to share their views, professional articles, creative ideas, or artist statements. That is how they are becoming more approachable to the audience, who are welcome to discuss topics.

The founder of our organization and creator of the whole idea is Smiljana AntiÄ‡. Self-taught artist, Smiljana has been curating art for about a decade now, starting with a gallery in Belgarde. She also took the time to jury multiple shows, her passion for art being so great.

After curating at the gallery for just over a year, she took the time to pursue her own art again while interning for KC Grad beside the executive director.

She then took another break while having her first daughter, but found herself missing her involvement as both an artist and a curator greatly, so she began curating at a local coffee shop for two years, gaining the respect of many artists and others related to the field. After censorship issues and stepping down from the position there, she partnered up with Amanda Thoron Walton and developed what is now known as Art Market Serbia, with Smiljana heading the operations of the online gallery herself.

They opened their "doors" on September 14, 2019; just a month shy of meeting and partnering. Both Amanda and Smiljana are artists and curators, so they understand the whole artistic cycle, together with the needs of both artists and spectators. Their life experience helped them to create a self-sustaining organization, whose purpose is to liberate and support the local art scene.
